# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 2026*

You're already paying for Spotify. This is a free tool to get more out of it. We don't collect, store, or monetize your data.

## What We Access

This app accesses your Spotify account data through Spotify's official API with your explicit permission. We access:

- Your playlist library (names, track lists)
- Your liked songs
- Your playback queue (for queue shuffle)
- Basic profile info (username)

## What We Store

We store your Spotify access tokens in an encrypted server-side session cookie. We do NOT store:

- Your Spotify password
- Your listening history
- Any personal data beyond your username
- Any data on third-party servers

## Internal Token

The optional "internal token" you provide (for personalized playlist access) is stored only in your encrypted session and is never logged, shared, or persisted beyond the session.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party. All processing happens between your browser and Spotify's API.

## Cookies

We use a single encrypted session cookie to maintain your login. No tracking cookies, no analytics cookies.

## Your Rights

You can revoke this app's access at any time via your Spotify account settings at [spotify.com/account/apps](https://www.spotify.com/account/apps/).

## Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the app maintainer via the [project repository](https://github.com/jmlgr/spotifyscripts/issues).
